# PRD.md

## Problem

Parents and gift buyers in India need an easy way to browse and purchase baby dresses online without dealing with cluttered interfaces or poor product discovery experiences.

## Target User

* Parents shopping for babies and toddlers.
* Family members purchasing gifts for newborns and young children.
* Customers across India looking for convenient online shopping.

## Core Features (ranked)

1. **Product browsing experience**

   * Fast loading product catalog.
   * Category-based browsing.
   * Product search and filtering.

2. **Product detail pages**

   * Multiple images.
   * Size selection.
   * Product descriptions and pricing.

3. **Shopping cart**

   * Add, remove, and update quantities.
   * Cart persistence during browsing.

4. **Checkout flow**

   * Shipping address collection.
   * Order summary.
   * Payment processing.

5. **Razorpay payment integration**

   * Secure payment processing.
   * Payment success and failure handling.

6. **Multi-language support**

   * Ability to switch between languages for key storefront pages.

## Out of Scope

* Customer reviews and ratings.
* Wishlist functionality.
* Loyalty or rewards programs.
* AI product recommendations.
* Advanced business analytics.
* Multi-vendor marketplace support.

## Success Criteria

* Users can discover products within a few clicks.
* Users can complete an order from browsing to payment without assistance.
* Product pages load quickly and display correctly on mobile devices.
* Multi-language switching works across core customer-facing pages.
* Demo successfully demonstrates a complete ecommerce flow.
